The Problem

Due to the Covid-19 pandemic, it was getting difficult for a defence aspirant to prepare from home, for a crucial test called WAT for the SSB. They needed a digital platform that helps them prepare for the same.

The Solution

A web application where an individual can take WAT test simulation, which will help them to get familiar with the process and thus boost up their performance.
One can download question set required for WAT with just a click, which currently is a tedious task and requires considerable amount of human time and effort.
Aspirants can assess their WAT responses online with our AI assessor, this will help them acknowledge their progress for the preparation of SSB.
